Hi, I'm Cass, and I code.

Let's make something awesome!

I am an energetic, driven, and detail-oriented software engineer interested in user accessibility. I specialize in creating apps and websites that help people in as many ways as possible. I'm a queer nonbinary trans black biracial Latinx person, so intersectionality is important to me. I want to bring my skills to a tech company that creates products to help marginalized people thrive. Outside of coding, I love playing video games, reading, and snuggling with my cat when he lets me.
